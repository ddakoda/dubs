A little bit about me.
I would start my story on a small island in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. But this story would include many leaps and bounds and airline tickets, which got me to where I am today. Most of those leaps, were spent trying my hand in many varied roles, within the tech world.
I like to believe that we all have the ability to inspire those around us, with whatever tools we have. My tools just so happen to be development and design. But at the end of the day, I am a story teller. Using my keyboard to craft small pieces of functional art work.
Put simply, I am the girl at your local health foods store contemplating every bottle of hot sauce. Wondering who the people are behind the bottle... I mean... I am also checking how much vinegar is in the recipe. Hot sauce is inspiring...
